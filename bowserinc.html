Bowser and Co.

While King and Bowser and his minions can be fierce and scary opponents, they do have their weaknesses! Hopefully the following guide can help you on your way through the Mushroom Kingdom to battle King Bowser

Goomba

You can kill the Goomba quite easily as he mindlessly marches forward until a permanent fixture redirects him back to where he came from, even toward fatal drops. Simply JUMP ON HIS HEAD!

Koopa

You can kill the Koopa quite easily as well, much like the Goomba he mindlessly marches forward until a permanent fixture redirects him back to where he came from, even toward fatal drops. Simply JUMP ON HIS SHELL, then launch it at another unsuspecting enemy!

Boo

The Boo shields its face if Mario or Luigi look at it, but it starts to chase Mario or Luigi if they look away. Boos are unable to be defeated unless the two are equipped with a Hammer Suit, Koopa shell, or Starman.

Pirahana Plant

Without being equipped with the fire or star power up you cannot kill this enemy and should avoid him as he will periodically dip and rise from his pipe. On occasion he may be gaurding a warp pipe!

Dry Bones

Dry Bones can be stomped on the head and turned into a pile of bones; however, they soon regenerate. Unlike normal Koopas, Dry Bones is immune to the tail of Raccoon Mario and the fireballs of Fire Mario but can be defeated with a Star or, if the player has access to one, a Hammer Suit.

BulletBill

With the right timing you can land on Bullet Bill and knock him out of the sky, but more often than not its best to duck and cover!

Bombomb

Walking angry explosives often dropped from the sky! A brave player can quickly scoop up a Bomb-omb and hurl it toward other enemies, but beware, they've a very short fuse!

Cheep Cheep

Cheep Cheeps are best avoided underwater, unless you have a Star power up, you will take Damage, or Die. Above land they can be seen launching out of bodies of water and on a rare occasion getting themselves into bubbles and attacking. You can either jump on their heads, or burst their bubbles to defeat them! Avoid the cheep cheeps that emerge from Lava at all costs.

Blooper

Bloopers follow Mario in a zig zag pattern underwater and contact will harm Mario unless he has a Star Power Up. You can kill them with a fireball, but like most underwater enemies avoidance is a good tactic

Pokey

Pokeys first appear in Super Mario Bros. 2 and will slowly chase after the player. In this game, Pokeys are green and can be defeated by throwing objects at their body segments, or picking up one segment at a time.

ShyGuy

ShyGuy's come in a variety of colors Red Shy Guys walk off ledges while Pink ones don't, striking a resemblance to Green and Red Koopa Troopas respectively, although the primary difference is that Red Shy Guys are known to turn in the direction of the player after landing.They can be easily disposed by being picked up and thrown against another enemy or into a nearby abyss. They can also be defeated by having an object thrown at them.

ChainChomp

The Chain Chomps are attached to Wooden Blocks and try to lunge at Mario. If they tug on their chain 50 times or if the timer hits 160 seconds, all Chain Chomps come loose. They return in the remake, Super Mario Advance 4: Super Mario Bros. 3. There are four ways to defeat Chain Chomps in this game: using a Starman, throwing a Koopa Shell, throwing a hammer at it as Hammer Mario, and stomping on it as a statue with Tanooki Mario.
